Vectorised Photography

Low detail level

These are examples of photographs I've taken, converted to Scalable Vector Graphics (SVG) format using primitive. The use of vector graphics means that they can be rescaled to any size without becoming pixelated.

These examples use a low level of detail and therefore take very little storage. I've tried to retain enough detail to be representative of the original image while still being somewhat abstract.

See also high level of detail images.
