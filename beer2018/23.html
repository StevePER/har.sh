Bodriggy Chuckaboo White IPA

23

Chuckaboo White IPA
Bodriggy Brewing
Victoria
355mL bottle
5.5% Alc/Vol

The Beer

This cloudy IPA features a massive whiff of hop aromas of candied orange, peach and tropical fruit with just a hint of spice from added coriander seed. 40 kg of fresh oranges make their way into this balanced IPA by way of our custom built hop-back, giving this beer a fresh fruity flavour and resiny bitterness.

The Brewery

Bodriggy's beer is shameless. Brewed with false oaths, a single wild idea & vainglory. Much like the man himself.

Beer Fact of the Day

Beer was illegal in Iceland until March 1, 1989. Now, the date is celebrated every year in Reykjavik as Bjordagur, or Beer Day.
