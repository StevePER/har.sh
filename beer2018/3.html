The Beer

Rich roasted malts are the star attraction when it comes to our deep, dark porter. It's brewed with chocolate malts for their cocoa and coffee notes and then sweeter crystal malts along with rolled oats to soften the flavour. A combination of classic noble hop varieties and Australian grown Ella balance the malt with a gentle bitterness and a complimentary light tropical and spice aroma. Big and bold but not overpowering, it's the perfect match for hearty, fire-kissed meats or dark chocolate desserts.

The Brewery

Colonial Brewing Company opened in 2004, and was one of the first breweries in the Margaret River region. In 2006 the brewery was awarded “Champion Small Brewery” at the Australian International Beer Awards. In 2007 they went on to win the Champion Australian Brewery award. In 2015 Colonial opened a second brewery in Port Melbourne. The current head brewer is Justin Fox, who previously worked for the Swan Brewery and The Generous Squire.
