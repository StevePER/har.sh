The Beer

A super-clean malt profile allows the ship-load of juicy tropical fruit flavours to arrive on the desert island of your palate unhindered. And it comes in a can, so after you Krush it, you can Krush it.

At 4.7%, Kaiju Krush is Kaiju's lowest alcohol beer. “We just wanted to have a beer that you could give to the inlaws and they'll be happy and they'll want to have a couple.”

The Brewery

We've been into making (and drinking) beer for a long time — we're kind of nerdy scientists about it actually. In late 2013 we decided to start making beer professionally and released a Double IPA that was a little bit hoppy and got some attention.

There's three of us - Nat, Callum and Clara. We are all giant nerds and look after different aspects of our craft.

We do everything by hand. Sometimes this is painful because we're perfectionists and our methods aren't commercial, but we love it and there's no other way to get the flavours we want.

Beer Fact of the Day

Norway's first aircraft hijacking took place in June 1985. The hijacker, apparently fed-up with society, demanded to speak with the Prime Minister and the Minister of Justice of Norway but surrendered his pistol after an hour in exchange for more beer.
