The Beer

We pushed the complex and citrus-heavy flavors of hops to a new level with this orange-infused IPA. Combining bright and bold hops with real orange in the fermenter creates layers of citrus-crazy flavors, yet it maintains a balance and a crisp hop bite that make it the perfect aromatic IPA.

The Brewery

Sierra Nevada Brewing was founded in 1979 when founders Ken Grossman and Paul Camusi expanded their homebrewing hobby into a brewery in Chico, California. With $50,000 in loans from friends and family, Grossman and Camusi rented a (280 m²) warehouse and pieced together discarded dairy equipment and scrapyard metal to create their brewing equipment. They were later able to acquire second-hand copper brewing kettles from Germany before moving to their larger, current brewing facility in 1989.

Sierra Nevada has now grown to be the seventh-largest brewing company in the United States.
